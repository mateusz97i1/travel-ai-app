# 🌍 TravelAI

**TravelAI** is an intelligent travel planning assistant designed to make dream vacations simple, exciting, and stress-free. By leveraging smart algorithms, the app transforms the way users plan their journeys—moving from hours of manual research to instant, personalized itineraries.

* **Smart Itinerary Generation:** Instantly designs a personalized travel plan based on your chosen destination and trip length.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Backend:** [Django](https://www.djangoproject.com/) (Python)
* **Frontend:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
* **Template Engine:** Django Templates (Inheritance & Custom Blocks)
* **UI Elements:** Modern gradient buttons, shadow-sm cards, and interactive grid layouts.

---

## 📋 How It Works

1.  **Select Destination:** Pick your next stop.
2.  **Set Duration:** Define your trip length.
3.  **Generate:** Hit **"Generate plan"** to receive a curated, step-by-step itinerary optimized just for you.
